Game Description: This game has you following the story of an extinct pterodactyl who has been revived on another planet. Trapped inside a mad world and trying to escape, you are flying through a series of rigid mazes filled with lava spikes that can kill you upon contact. You must avoid these and reach the end. Collect coins to increase your score; the pink and blue colored triangular ones will change your speed! Try to beat the game with the highest score and the least death count, which are recorded in your Scoreboard!

Game Genre: This is a 2D platformer game that utilizes diagonal flying mechanics.
